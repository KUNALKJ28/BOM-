Tungarli Dam

June 22, 2025

Nestled in the hills of Lonavala, Tungarli Dam comes alive during the monsoon. Surrounded by rolling green fields, rugged stone walls, and mist-covered mountains, it’s a quiet escape into nature’s embrace. With cool winds, dramatic clouds, and a view that soothes the soul, Tungarli isn’t just a dam—it’s a reminder of nature’s calm, just a short ride away.

Daman Bike Ride

June 17, 2025

On the occasion of Maharashtra Day, passionate bikers from across the state came together for a special commemorative ride. With flags fluttering and engines roaring, the riders paid tribute to the spirit, culture, and pride of Maharashtra. Dressed in unity and riding in formation, they showcased discipline, brotherhood, and respect for the rich heritage of the state. The ride not only celebrated Maharashtra’s formation but also promoted road safety and responsible riding.

Maharashtra Din Bike Rider
