Founder

Kunal Mestry (popularly known as Mumbiker Kunal) With over 15 years of riding experience, Kunal began his journey by planning line-ups and managing marshalling duties for various biking groups. In 2018, he took a visionary step and founded the Brotherhood of Mumbai—a riding unit born from a group of like-minded biker friends. What started as a small initiative has now grown into a well-known name in Mumbai’s biking scene, thanks to its unique and engaging ride experiences. Through this platform, Kunal has successfully organized several major tours, including the challenging terrains of Ladakh and the Spiti circuit, scenic Konkan coastal routes, Hampi explorations, and many more short and long-distance rides.
